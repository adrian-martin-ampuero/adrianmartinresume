Why? There's already the resume


That's the thing: This is not a resume. The reason I built this website is because sometimes, a one page resume doesn't paint the full picture of who a person truly is. It may outline the milestones, the professional achievements, and the educational background, but it often misses the essence—the real human behind the accolades.

That's why I believe in going beyond the paper, in sharing more about who I am, the reasons behind the decisions I've made, and what I'm passionate about, including my projects. So, let's dive deeper and get to know each other a bit more.

Experience

Space Systems Engineering intern

German Aerospace Center - DLR

April-September 2024 in Bremen, Germany

I will be joining the German Aerospace Center (DLR) to work on my thesis, which marks the culmination of my master's program. My career ambition is completely focused on space systems, and the chance to contribute at such a prestigious institution was an opportunity I simply could not pass up.

At DLR, I will be engaged in analyzing mission requirements, exploring a requirements traceability concept from the system level down to the domain, applied to an actual mission.

Design Engineer Intern

Piedrafita Systems

September-November 2022 in Madrid, Spain

Piedrafita is a small company specializing in the mobility of combat vehicles. In my first internship there, I worked on a project named HS3, a rotary shock absorber that would be fitted onto tanks. I undertook this job with great enthusiasm and learned not only a great deal about CATIA but also got to experience the "real" side of engineering.

My days were spent moving between the design office and the workshop, allowing me to see firsthand the practical application of engineering principles.

Program Coordinator

Program of July 2018 in Bray, Ireland

I used to attend a summer camp in Ireland every year to learn English, as it involved spending the month of July living with a host family and culminated in taking a Trinity level examination at the end of the month.
